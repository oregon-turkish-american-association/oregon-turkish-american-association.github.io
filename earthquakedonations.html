ORTA Earthquake Donation Campaign
February 2023

Please offer your helping hand to the people affected by the earthquake.




    As Oregon Turkish American Association (ORTA), we are deeply saddened by the multiple earthquake(s) that affected the southeastern part of Türkiye and the surrounding region. The first of the earthquakes was measured at a magnitude of 7.8 on February 6th, at 4:17 am local time. What makes this disaster unprecedented is the fact that it was followed by another 7.5 magnitude quake only after 9 hours of the initial tremor that shocked people in the middle of the night. Widespread damage with more than 3400 people killed and thousands more injured have been reported so far. With the severe winter conditions in the region, the death toll is expected to increase. Our thoughts are with the injured and the families of the victims. Türkiye's Disaster and Emergency Management Agency (AFAD) on Monday called on the international community for help "in the field of urban search and rescue", declaring a fourth-level state of alert.

    Please donate to support ORTA’s earthquake relief fund to contribute to the healing of wounds in our country. Donations made to ORTA are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by IRS Internal Revenue Code 501(c)3. We encourage our donors to explore matching options that might be available via their company workplace benefits or other private donors.

    Proceedings from the campaign will be passed to Bridge to Turkiye Fund (BTF).
